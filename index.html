What is BSM?

BSM stands for(Ba Sith Mount) motobike team that is invented by people who lived in the location of Basith Mountain. It is a peronal motobike team that we take a ride for ralaxing including OFF and ON road in Cambodia.

Detail

BSM is created on 29-May-2019 By Mr. BUNTHOEUN VORN. It's first start with at least 7 members with 6 motobike.

Present

Until present, we had more than 5 traveling with team in Cambodia including Pursat(VealVeng), KompongSpeu(ChomkarKroch Waterfall), Kompot(Bokor), Kep(Beach), PreahSihanouk(Tabarang Beach), SiemReap(Angkor Wat)...

Future

In the future, BSM will have not only motobike, but we will have vehicle team trip wtih 4 wheels car including OFF/ON raod trip.
